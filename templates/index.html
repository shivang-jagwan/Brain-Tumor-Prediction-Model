This system helps you detect brain tumors from MRI scans instantly.

About the Project

This system uses deep learning to classify brain MRI scans into 4 categories:

  • Glioma Tumor
  • Meningioma Tumor
  • Pituitary Tumor
  • No Tumor

The model was trained on thousands of MRI scans to achieve high accuracy in tumor detection.
